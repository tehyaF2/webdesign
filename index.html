Pumpkin spice french toast recipie

ingredients needed!

  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk-(whole,low-fat,or skim)
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 1/2 tsp vinilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 12 slices texas toast- or french bread, or challah, or croissant halves

whipped pumpkin butter ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted or unsalted butter
    • (1 stick), room temp
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
  • 3 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tsp vinilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

how to make pumpkin french toast

  1. set a large non-stick skillet set over medium heat, add a small amount of butter and melt.
  2. in a rectangular baking dish, whist together the eggs, milk, pumpkin puree, vinilla, cinnamon and pumpkin pie spice
  3. dip the bread into the mixture, flip sides and then place on the hot skillet. cook until each side is golden brown, about 2-3 minutes on each side. repeat with remaining ingredients, serve hot with whipped pumpkin butter, maple syrup and toasted chopped nuts

how to make pumpkin butter

  1. in a small mixing bowl, add the softened butter and beat with a hand mixer for 2 minutes or until light and fluffy
  2. add the pumpkin puree, maple syrup or honey, vinilla, cinnamon and pumpkin pie spice. beat for an additional minute or until fluffy.
  3. store in an air-tight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

notes

  • feel free to use honey or syrup!- if you like you're french toast on the sweeter side, add 1 tbsp of brown sugar to the mixture
